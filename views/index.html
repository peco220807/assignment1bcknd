About the Project

DEZIRE is a digital fashion showcase inspired by modern retail aesthetics. The platform introduces clean, curated clothing categories and will evolve into a full online store experience. Our goal is to provide a stylish and intuitive space to explore contemporary fashion.

Planned Features

  • Category pages with detailed product listings
  • Search and filters (size, color, price)
  • Product detail pages
  • Favorites system
  • Lightweight user profile
  • Shopping cart system
